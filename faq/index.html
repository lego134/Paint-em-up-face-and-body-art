FAQ

What kind of products do you use?
I only use professional FDA-approved face paints and cosmetic glitter. I never use acrylic craft paint or craft glitter as they are not meant to be used on skin and could cause harm to skin or eyes.
Do you book birthday parties?
Unfortunately, at this time I am unable to book birthday parties.
What kind of events do you paint?
I paint at festivals, or any event that offers a Pay Per Face (PPF) option. Prices are adjusted according to type of event, ie, family fun events.
What areas do you service?
I currently serve Fremont County, but am available to travel outside the area.
